Housing Benefit Assessment Guide (BEN-POL-001 v6.0)

New claimants must claim Universal Credit via DWP. Housing Benefit continues only for: pensioners, specified exempt accommodation, temporary accommodation, existing legacy claimants.

Capital limit: GBP 16,000 (working age). Income taper: 65p reduction per pound above applicable amount.

Overpayment recovery: official error may restrict recovery. Claimant error - full recovery applies. Maximum deduction 10 percent of applicable amount.

Local Housing Allowance rates updated April 2026. Under 35 claimants entitled to shared accommodation rate only unless exempt.
