Grievance Policy and Procedure (HR-POL-001 v5.0)

Informal resolution should be attempted first before formal grievance.

Formal grievance timeframes: Acknowledge within 5 working days. Hearing within 15 working days. Decision within 5 working days of hearing. Appeal within 10 working days of decision.

Employee has right to be accompanied by trade union representative or work colleague at hearing.

All proceedings are confidential. Breach of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action.
