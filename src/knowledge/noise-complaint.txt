Noise Complaint Procedure (ENV-POL-001 v4.0)

Log all noise complaints within 1 working hour. Acknowledge to resident within 1 working day with reference number.

Statutory Nuisance threshold under Environmental Protection Act 1990 Section 79: unreasonably and substantially interferes with use and enjoyment of property, or is prejudicial to health.

Out-of-hours service available Friday and Saturday 8pm to 3am. Direct residents to Metropolitan Police on 101 outside these hours.

Issue noise diary sheets to complainants. Serve Abatement Notice under Section 80 when statutory nuisance confirmed.
