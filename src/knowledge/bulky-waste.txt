Bulky Waste Collection Policy (ENV-POL-002 v2.0)

Accepted items: furniture, white goods, mattresses, garden furniture, exercise equipment.

Not accepted: asbestos, clinical waste, tyres, construction waste, hazardous chemicals.

Free collection eligibility: residents on means-tested benefits, residents aged 75 or over, registered disabled residents. One free collection per calendar year.

Items must be presented at agreed collection point by 7am on collection day. Minimum 5 working days notice required for booking.
